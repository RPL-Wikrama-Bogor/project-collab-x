Tulip

Tulips are one of the most popular and beautiful flowers in the world. They are known for their bright, colorful petals and simple, elegant shape. Tulips grow from bulbs and usually bloom in the spring. They come in many colors, such as red, yellow, pink, purple, and white.

Rose

The rose is a woody perennial that was originally from China but is now grown across the world.It is characterised by wide range of colours and sizes.Roses are multi-petal flowers available in an array of colours.Some varieties are known for their prickles along the stems of the plant.

Lavender

Lavender is a small, purple flower known for its sweet, calming scent. It grows on a bush with long stems and narrow, grey-green leaves. Lavender is native to the Mediterranean region, but now it is grown all over the world. People love lavender for its fragrance and many uses. It is often used in perfumes, soaps, oils, and even in cooking. Lavender oil is popular in aromatherapy because it helps people relax, reduce stress, and sleep better.

Sunflower

Sunflowers are tall, bright flowers known for their large, round yellow petals and dark centers. They are called "sunflowers" because they look like the sun and often turn their heads to follow the sun across the sky. This movement is called heliotropism.

Jasmine

Jasmine is a small, white flower known for its sweet and strong fragrance. It grows on a bush or vine and usually blooms at night or early in the morning. Jasmine is popular in many warm countries, including Indonesia, India, and parts of the Middle East.
